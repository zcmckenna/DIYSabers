About

All of DIYSabers parts were designed to fit around a piece of 1" PVC pipe. This allows endless customizability and allows you, the user, to make the saber of your dreams. All you have to do is browse our catalogue of free 3D part files and choose the ones you like, then download them. From there you can print your files in whatever colors or materials you would like, or paint them, and then assemble your saber... It's as simple as that. Make sure to check back regularly for our ever growing inventory of parts.
