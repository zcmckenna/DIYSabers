Getting Started

Before you can complete your saber you will need to do a few more things. First off, you will need a blade for your new saber. We recommend the blade found here as it is the blade we designed the emitters for. Also to add sound we recommend Arduino.cc because it is open source and easy to customize. Other than that, just get a piece od 1" ID PVC pipe and start building!
